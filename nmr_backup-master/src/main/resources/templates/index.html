about us

Explore the difference

Nordic Motorhome Rental had its humble beginning in March 2019, and has since drastically expanded its business to being the biggest RV rental company in Scandinavia.

The fleet is currently consisting of no less than 32 motorhomes, varying from tiny 2-person campervans to massive luxury motorhomes with up to 6 beds.

Two prominent business men, Douglas Beaver and Saifullah Jabbar, are in charge of the company and have ruthlessly been defeating competing businesses.
